Child Safety Standards

GeoDrop is committed to protecting children. This page describes our externally published safety standards under the Google Play Child Safety Standards Policy.

1. Zero Tolerance
GeoDrop has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE), child sexual abuse material (CSAM), and grooming targeting minors. Such content or behaviour is removed immediately and reported to authorities.

2. Age Requirement
GeoDrop is for users aged 13 and above only. Use by children under 13 is prohibited.

3. User Content Moderation
• All drops are subject to reporting and moderation.
• In-app report feature is available on every drop.
• Content targeting children is prioritised for review.

4. Technical Safeguards
Automated and manual moderation, abuse detection, blocking and access restrictions.

5. Legal Compliance
When CSAE/CSAM is detected, we report to law enforcement and authorised bodies (e.g. NCMEC and local equivalents).

6. Reporting
Report child safety violations in-app or via the email below.

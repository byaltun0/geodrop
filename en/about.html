About

GeoDrop v1.4.0

GeoDrop is a location-based social discovery app for leaving and finding anonymous messages near you.

Features:
• Discover nearby drops on map & radar
• Anonymous profile: XP, levels, badges
• Create, like and share drops
• Social hub and leaderboard
• 4 languages: Turkish, English, Ukrainian, Russian
